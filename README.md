### Email Section

The **Contact** section of my portfolio includes an email form where you can directly send your feedback. Follow these simple steps:

1. **Locate the Contact Section**: Scroll to the bottom of the homepage.
   
2. **Fill Out the Form**: 
   - **Your Name**: Enter your name.
   - **Your Email**: Provide a valid email address so I can get back to you.
   - **Message**: Share your thoughts, suggestions, or inquiries in the message box.

3. **Send Your Message**: Once you’re satisfied with your feedback, click on the **Send** button.
